about us

(re)vision organizing is here to help you organize your home to make the most of space, streamline your home, and help create a system that works for you and your family.

(re)vision was born from the idea of finding a new vision or “re”-visioning the space you have, while also editing and revising your stuff as your needs continue to change and evolve.

Our team is small but mighty with Meg - the owner of (re)vision. She’s a self-starter, trained with the Professional Organizers of Canada, is passionate about efficiency, helping others, and owns way too many label makers.

Currently serving Kitchener, Waterloo, Cambridge, Guelph and surrounding areas.

our method

(re)view

First we’ll review the space and work through how you use your space, how you want to use your space and come up with some ideas on how to make it function the way you envisioned.

(re)fine

Next we refine your stuff by sorting and filtering out the things you no longer want or need. We'll organize the items to make your space more functional and efficient. Remember we are organizing for real life!

(re)visit

Finally, we revisit the system - do the changes make sense? How does the change make you feel? This is a system that will continue to evolve and change, just like you and your needs.
