about us

Servering Kitchener, Waterloo, Cambridge and surrounding areas. (re)vision professional home organizing looks to help you organize your home in a way that supports your lifestyle. We offer a number of services to help you find joy in your space again.

kitchen

No one thinks they have enough room in their kitchens these days. Kitchens are one of the most important rooms to have a system of organization with items coming in and out every day. We can help find a system that work for you and find an efficient use of your space.

basement

Basements are so often forgotten, and give you a *cringing* feeling every time you go downstairs. But there is SO MUCH SPACE down there! Let us help you get the most out of your space and take away that *cringy* feeling.

closet

From a whole new closet system, to tidying drawers, and everything inbetween. You start every day in your closet, time to start your day on the right foot!

office

Do you work from home? Let us help you make it an efficient space to let you do what do you best with ease. We can help you organize your paperwork, set up your desk space, find efficiencies in your layout.

have another trouble area of your home?

(re)view

The first step is to identify the opportunity! Let's think about how you'd like to use your space and come up with some ideas.

(re)fine

Next we sort like items and filter out the things you no longer want or need. We'll organize and contain the items to make your space more functionaly and efficient. Remember we are organizing for real life!

(re)visit

Finally, we review. Do the changes make sense, and how do they make you feel? It's not a destination, but a system that will continue to evolve and change, just like you.
